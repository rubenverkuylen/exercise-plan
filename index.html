Weekly structure

General structure per week
  • 3 Days/Week (Gym + Running):
    • Full workout (~45–60 min) stretches)
  • Daily (Optional 15 min):
    • Core activation, HIIT, or mobility stretches)

Weekly template

Day 1 – Full-Body Strength + HIIT Core Finisher
  • Warm-up:
    • 5–10 min (rower/treadmill + dynamic stretches)
  • Strength:
    • Squat – 4x8
    • Bent-over row – 3x10
    • Dumbbell chest press – 3x10
    • Walking lunges – 3x10/leg
  • Core Finisher (10 min AMRAP):
    • 20 Russian twists
    • 15 mountain climbers
    • 10 leg raises
    • 5 burpees
Day 2 – Low-Intensity Cardio
  • Run or brisk walk for 30–40 minutes.
Day 3 – Rest or Short Core (15 min)
  • Planks, leg raises, mountain climbers, or mobility exercises.
Day 4 – Upper Body + Core
  • Strength:
    • Pull-ups or lat pulldown – 3x8
    • Dumbbell shoulder press – 3x10
    • Plank rows – 3x10/side
    • Bicep curls & tricep extensions – 2x12 each
  • Core Circuit:
    • Plank (30–60s)
    • Hanging leg raises or knee tucks – 3x10
    • Cable or banded woodchoppers – 3x10/side
Day 5 – Optional HIIT Cardio
  • 30s sprint / 60s walk x 10 rounds (15–20 minutes total).
Day 6 – Lower Body Strength + Core
  • Strength:
    • Hip Thrusts – 3x15
    • Bulgarian split squat – 3x8/leg
    • Glute bridges – 3x15
    • Calf raises – 3x15
  • Core Blast (10 min EMOM):
    • Minute 1: 30s V-ups + 30s plank
    • Minute 2: 40s bicycle crunch + 20s rest
Day 7 – Rest or Mobility/Yoga
  • Light mobility work, yoga, or a walk.

Daily (15-Minute Sessions)

Pick one daily
  • Core (e.g. plank variations, leg raises, mountain climbers)
  • HIIT (e.g. 20s on/10s off burpees, jump squats, high knees)
  • Mobility/Yoga (hips, spine, hamstrings)
